How It Works

Six steps.
Under three minutes.

From pulling up to pulling in. First time takes under 3 minutes. Every time after that — under 15 seconds.

01

Search

Find parking near your destination before you leave. Filter by price, coverage, EV charging, and distance.

02

Reserve

Lock in your spot in one tap. Pay with Apple Pay, Google Pay, or your Parkt Wallet. Your spot is held.

03

Navigate

Get entrance-level directions — not just the address. Parkt takes you to the right door, right gate.

04

Access

Scan your QR at the gate or let the camera read your plate. The arm goes up. Walk in.

05

Park

Your spot is confirmed. Park with confidence knowing it's yours for the duration you reserved.

06

Pay

Already done. Payment was taken at reservation. Leave whenever you want — no checkout required.
